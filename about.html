Meet Your Photographer

Hi, I'm Faith! I live in Jackson, Tennnessee and attend school, participate in cheer, all while balancing a photography business. I've been into photography since I was thirteen and I really do enjoy it. I've had photoshopping experience for six years and I had the oppurtunity to learn more about it in a photoshopping class during a semester of my freshman year. Other than that, I am completely self taught. My style is more unique than others and I try to create a daydream scene with everyone of my shoots. My goal is to bring out a side of my clients that you don't see often. Their "raw side" and how God created them. Everyone is beautiful in their own way!
