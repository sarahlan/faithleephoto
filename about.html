Meet Your Photographer

Hi, I'm Faith. I live in Jackson, Tennnessee and attend school along with cheer, while blanceing a photography buness with that. I've been into photography since I was thirteen and I really do enjoy it. I've had photoshopping experience for six years and I had the oppertunity to learn more about it in a photoshopping class during a semester of my Freshman, but other then that I am completely self taught. My goal is to bring out a side of my clients that you don't see often "thier raw side" of how God created them. Everyone is beautiful in their own way! My style is more unique than others I think and I try to create a daydream scene with everyone of my shoots.
