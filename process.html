What To Expect

Each shoot is different and I love to get to know my models personally beforehand. This creates a comfortable and fun environment for the both of us and allows them to express themself without fear of judgement. I mainly do portrait sessions, but I've done couples and family shoots as well. I typically go downtown or to a forest scenery for photoshoots but, if you have your own ideas, I'm opened to suggestions and am always up for a challenge! A typical photoshoot will take around an hour and the cost depends on the number of people, outfit changed, and hours. I can give you a general estimate once we begin planning your shoot. My main goal is that you have fun being a model for a day and to see how truly beautiful you are!
